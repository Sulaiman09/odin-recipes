Creamy Garlic-Parmesan Mashed Potatoes

Classic and perfected mashed potatoes

Tired of plain mashed potatoes? Why not try adding something new?

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cups 2% milk
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
  2. While potatoes are cooking, melt butter over medium-low heat in a small saucepan. Add garlic and saute until soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes and return to the pot. Add garlic-butter mixture, milk, Parmesan cheese, parsley, sea salt, and pepper; mash until smooth.
